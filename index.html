Full Body Strength
🔥 Warm-Up — 5 min · via Bend
Bend Full Body Warm-Up ▼
  • 1
    March in Place — 30s · lift knees gently, get blood flowing first
  • 2
    Step Jacks — 30s · step one foot out to side, bring other to meet it, no jumping
  • 3
    Arm Circles — 30s · arms out to sides, small to big circles, then reverse
  • 4
    Arm Swings — 30s · swing arms forward across chest then back out wide
  • 5
    Trunk Twist — 30s · arms out at shoulder height, twist side to side, hips forward
  • 6
    Toe Touch Twist — 30s · bend forward, touch opposite toe with opposite hand
  • 7
    Knee Hugs — 30s · lift one knee to chest, hug it in, alternate legs
  • 8
    Leg Swings — 30s · hold wall, swing leg forward and back, then switch
  • 9
    Knee Circles — 30s · hands on knees, small circles both directions

Bow Stance — Left
3 × 30s · left foot forward · 45s rest
Bottom Push-Up Hold
1 × 60s hold · build toward full 60s · chest just above floor
Deep Squat Hold
1 × 60s hold · build toward full 60s · go shallow, hold wall
Plank Hold
3 × 30s · 45s rest
Bird-Dog Hold — Right
3 × 30s · right arm + left leg · hold still · 20s rest
Bird-Dog Hold — Left
3 × 30s · left arm + right leg · hold still · 45s rest
Prone YTW
3 × 8 each position · 45s rest · lie face down, lift arms into Y, T then W shape

  • 9

  • 7

  • 12
    Spinal Twist — Right — 60s · drop right knee across body, left arm out, shoulders flat
